    Ingredients Required ::--

  • bread slices
  • butter for spreading,brushing,cooking

  • stuffing material

    • chopped onion,chooped tomatoes,chooped coriander(dhania)
    • crushed boil potatos
    • mirchi powder
    • chooped chilli
  • cummin seeds
  • salt
  • dhania powder

    Follow the steps to prepare sandwich :

  1. Prepare stuff
  2. Mix crushed boil potatos,onion,tomatos,coriander and mix mirchi,dhania powder
  3. Take pan add some butter into it
  4. After butter is heated add cummin seeds
  5. Then add prepared stuff into pan for 5 min
  6. After that take two slice of bread brush butter on it
  7. Put stuff on the bread slice and put another bread on it
  8. After this heat the stuffed bread pair on tawa till it getted red from both side
  9. Now cut the bread slice into triangular shape
  10. here is your veg tawa sandwich redy to serve
